Why Rust?

Terracotta and rust tones are associated with land, earth, and legacy — the material foundation of what Seek Research works with every day. It brings warmth without softness, urgency without alarm.

Why Gold?

Not metallic, not flashy — a muted, aged gold that speaks to inheritance and value recovered. It marks the moments that matter: headlines, key figures, successful resolutions.

Why Ink + Parchment?

The combination evokes the physical world of documents, deeds, and legal records — the actual artifacts Seek Research works with. Dark and warm, never cool or sterile.

Why Sage?

A quiet supporting tone for backgrounds, borders, and secondary elements. Sage grounds the palette in something organic and human — a nod to communities and neighborhoods being revitalized.
